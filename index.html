The Process

How to Order

Pre-orders only · Pickup in the Tyndall area · Minimum 3pc per flavour

1

Choose Your Flavours

Browse our flavours including the Flavour of the Month. Select quantities in multiples of 3 — minimum 3pc per flavour. Need more than 24 pieces? Slide into our DMs on Instagram!

2

Submit Your Order

Fill out the order form with your details, preferred pickup date, and payment method. Wednesdays are our regular pickup day — order cutoff is Sunday at 7PM the week before. Orders after cutoff are automatically moved to the following Wednesday.

3

Get Confirmed

Once your order is in, secure your spot with e-transfer or cash. Non-Wednesday pickups are subject to Shai's confirmation. Need it sooner? DM Shai on Instagram.

4

Pick Up & Enjoy

Shai will reach out on pickup day to coordinate a time. Pick up your freshly baked cookies in the Tyndall area and enjoy!
